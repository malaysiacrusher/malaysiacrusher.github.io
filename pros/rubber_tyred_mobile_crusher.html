Rubber Tyred Mobile Crusher Introduction

Rubber tyred mobile crusher, also known as rubber tyred mobile crushing station, is high-effective mobile crushing equipment with high performance. Rubber tyred mobile crusher can move on rugged roads and reduce material transporting costs, with effective operation, flexible combination, high adaptability, etc.

Applications of rubber tyred mobile crusher:

1. Rubber tyred mobile crusher is with a light weight and small size, so it’s ideal mobile crusher for working in narrow space;
2. Rubber tyred mobile crusher is easy to transport and equipped with multifunctional attachments, without damaging the road surface, with wide application range.
3. Rubber tyred mobile crusher has excellent rock fragmentation, high aggregate productivity;
4. Rubber tyred mobile crusher is available to form a powerful crushing line through combination with other equipment.
5. Rubber tyred mobile crusher is especially suitable for working in narrow sites and complex region.
