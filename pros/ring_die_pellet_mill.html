Ring Die Pellet Mill Introduction

Ring die pellet mill (also known as: ring die wood pellet machine), can directly suppress crushed corn, crushed soybean meal, crushed straw, crushed rice husks and other crushed materials into pellet feed. Ring die pellet mill is a kind of feed granulator equipment.

MCC produced the machine wood pellet machine price in the industry, guaranteed minimum case of equal quality are welcome.

Ring Die Pellet Mill Features

1. Ring die pellet mill covers a small area, with simple structure, wide adaptability and low noise.
2. Powdered materials and grass meal can be pelletized with a little liquid or without liquid, so the moisture of pellet feed is equal to that of powder before granulation, more suitable for storage.
3. The returns of mixed powdered feed are higher.
4. The feed pellets made by ring die pellet mill have higher hardness, smooth surface and cured inner, can improve nutrient digestion and absorption.
5. The feed pellets made by ring die pellet mill can reduce the adverse effect of digestion, kill parasite eggs and other pathogenic microorganisms, and reduce all kinds of pests and digestive system disease.

Ring Die Pellet Mill Working Principle

Ring die pellet mill uses the two screws on the eccentric gear in the middle of pinch roller to adjust the pressure; while flat die pellet mill uses screw thread center regulating mechanism to adjust the pressure, with jacking force of hundreds of tons, steady falling, gentle striking and even pressure. Rotation manual and automatic hydraulic adjusting modes can be used.

From the contrast between flat die pellet mill and ring die pellet mill, we can see that the pressure of flat die pellet mill is higher than ring die pellet mill, so flat die pellet mill is better for material like wood chips, straw and other materials with light weight and difficult in forming.
