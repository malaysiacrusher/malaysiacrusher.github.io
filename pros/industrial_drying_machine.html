Overview

Industrial Drying Machine Introduction

Industrial drying machine or industrial dryer is commonly used in dressing, metallurgy, construction, road, etc. There are many kinds of industrial dry mining equipments, such as three-cylinder dryer, river sand dryer, rotary dryer, etc. The coal consumption and power consumption of industrial dryer machine is lower 10-15% than that of ordinary industrial dryer, while the productivity is higher 10-15% than that of ordinary industrial dryer.

MCC is one of the most famous dryer manufacturers in China, and MCC dryers are with reliable quality, stable operation and reasonable price, so MCC is worth your trusted.

Industrial Drying Machine Features

1. Use multi-modular lifting devices, high thermal efficiency, low coal consumption.
2. Industrial drying equipment is suitable for a variety of furnace, high temperature boiling furnace, grinding powder coal stove and artificial add coal stove.
3. Transmission gear uses changeable pin column gear instead of traditional cast steel wheel to save investment cost and decrease the maintenance cost and time.
4. Industrial drying machine uses centralized control system, to achieve the micro computer monitoring and management of large enterprises.

Industrial Drying Machine Working Principle

The air sucked by the exhaust fan into the dryer gets heated through the heat exchanger; the wet materials inside the rotary dyer drum are turned over and over to change heat with the hot air. Then the moisture of the fabric is vaporized and ejected out from the dryer, so materials can be dried within a short time.
