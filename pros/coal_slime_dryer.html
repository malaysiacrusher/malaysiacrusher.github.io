Overview

Coal Slime Dryer Introduction

Coal slime dryer is also known as sludge dryer, suitable for drying slurry, coal slime, sludge, metal and nonmetal materials in coal mining industry. Slime has high humidity and high adhesion. Wet slime is sent into coal slime dryer via ribbon conveyor, and the massive coal slime will be sent into drying chamber of the sludge dryer.

Coal Slime Dryer Features

Coal slime dryer or sludge dryer has strong anti-overload capability, large quantity management, low fuel consumption and low drying costs. Discharging devices can prevent phenomenon of clogging of the feed drum dryer, to reduce the load of the exhaust system.

Coal Slime Dryer Working Principle

wet slime are sent into scattered equipment via ribbon conveyor, the massive coal slime after scattering are sent into drying chamber dryer of negative pressure, through feeding, cleaning, low temperature drying and discharging. The exhaust gas is drained after removing the dust, and the finished products are transported into warehouse.
