Quartz Crusher Introduction

Introduction to granite crusher

Cobble crusher, calcite crusher, granite crusher, basalt crusher, quartz crusher and dolomite crusher belong to rock crusher and they are all important mining crushers of our company, and they can be divided into jaw crusher, cone crusher, hammer crusher, impact crusher, etc. This kind of crusher not only crushes large chunks of materials, but also deals with materials whose edge lengths are below 100~500 mm.

Advantages of granite crusher

1. High crushing efficiency, small energy consumption

2. High crushing ratio, uniform granularity

3. Low cost

4. Stable structure, reliable performance

Crushing methods

1. Squeezing, crushers uses a pressing force to crush the material.

2. Splitting, crushers uses splitting power produced when tines wedge into materials to crush materials.

3. Bending, materials are bent and broken, like the two-point beam which stands concentrated load.
