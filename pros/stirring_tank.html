Overview

Agitation Tank Introduction

Agitation tank is made of stainless steel, with movable flaps for easy feeding. Fixed spindle speed and frequency control can be designed according to users’ requirement. Mixing barrel is with uniform mixing, no precipitation and other advantages.

Agitation tank is suitable for a variety of metal ores, mainly used for mixing before flotation to thoroughly mix pulp and pharmacy, as well as used for mixing other non-metallic minerals.

Agitation Tank Features

1. Compact structure and reliably sealing. Besides, there is an ash damper installed at the mouth of discharging gate, which can prevent water and flog to enter in.
2. Evenly mixing.
3. There are many rows of nozzles, which can guarantee that water can flow evenly.
4. There is a watershed set on the bottom, so the whole agitation tank is very clean.
5. Advanced equipment performance, stable quality of finished pulp, even mixing and quick discharging.

Agitation Tank Working Principle

When the agitation tank works, under the rotation of the impeller, the ore pulp and the reagents will have big and small circulation, and they will be evenly mixed, and the mixed suspension liquid will be discharged from the overflow port.
