Overview

Strong Briquetting Machine Introduction

MCC strong briquetting machine, also known as strong briquette machine, is mainly applied to doing cold ball pressing of coal powder, coal slime, coal, coke powder, coking coal, gold powder and refractory matter. All powder materials that need drawing up will be pressed by strong briquetting machine.

Strong Briquetting Machine" width="600" height="225" />

Strong Briquetting Machine Features

MCC strong briquette machine can be made flexibly by adjusting its pressure according to different ball pressing densities, and it is suitable for pressing materials with high hardness.

Strong Briquetting Machine Working Principle

1. When too many materials or metal blocks enter the space between the two compression rollers, the piston rod of the hydraulic cylinder gets so pressed that the pump will stop working and the energy accumulator will make buffer action facing pressure changing.

2. Then the flood valve starts the oil return and the piston rod replacement will widen the space between the rollers thus forcing hard materials to pass the rollers.

3. Finally, the whole system pressure returns to normal condition so that the rollers won’t be damaged. The strong briquetting machine can be made flexibly by adjusting its pressure according to different ball pressing density.
