Giant Cone Crushers will Be Launched in the Near Future

Date: 06-11-2014     From: MCC Machinery     Author: admin

Our company as one of the world's leading providers of mobile crushing and screening equipment has launched the new series of cone crushers recently, namely the largest addition to the highly successful range which includes a lot of different models.

In 2010, our company together with all the 500 staff moved into what we view as the next generation of mobile crushing and screening equipment. With the launch of our largest ever jaw crusher and largest ever screen, we are firmly positioning ourselves to meet the needs of the aggregates and mining segments. This time, the surprising addition of the new series of giant cone crushers to our range gives us one of the largest tracked mobile cone crushers on the market. This has been proved by the sales and marketing director of our company.

The new generation of products is specifically developed for larger users. The plant can accept an all-in feed from a primary crusher to produce up to 950 tph (1,047 US tph) of aggregate or mineral. Utilizing the latest international cone crusher technology, the operators of the cone crushers will benefit from hydraulic overload protection, hydraulic crusher setting adjustment, excellent product shape and a high reduction ratio. Further customer benefits provided by our company include a highly efficient crusher direct drive system, large feed hopper complete with metal detector and contaminated material purge facility.

For more information on the crushing, screening and washing products made and designed by our company, customers can go to our homepage to learn well.
