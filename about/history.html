Company History

Before 1987,the company was the State-owned the First Zhengzhou Machinery and Equipment Factory, major in producing mining machinery, combined the design and production of other machinery and equipment for nation-building projects. 

In 1987,the company was responsive to the needs of reform and opening, made asset consolidation and implemented reform, and established the “MCC Mining Machinery Co., Ltd.”, focusing on research and development, design and production of mining machinery for traditional dominant events. 

In 1990,the company combined with the coal information Institute of the State Administration of Quality and Technical Supervision, and became the product trial manufacturing base of latest technology of the Institute.

In 1994,backbone technical staff of MCC Machinery completed retraining at Coal Research Institute, and established “MCC mining machinery research and experimental base” in MCC company, paving the way for lifting equipment technology and production processes. 

In 1996,MCC Machinery carried out long-term cooperation with the 15th Engineering Bureau of China Railway Engineering, to provide equipment supply, technical guidance and maintenance work of stone crushing production line for the 15th Engineering Bureau of China Railway Engineering.

 

 

In 2002,MCC Machinery applied for settling in the high-tech development zone, Zhengzhou city, and been passed, and then began to build large-scale production workshop with 50,000 square meters. 

In 2003,the large-scale production plant covering an area of 50,000 square meters completed, and MCC Machinery went into its “Era in High-tech Zone”. 

In 2004,MCC Machinery held the activities of “Try to be the first to do something” and “put forward to ideas to promote production and ensure safety”, which greatly improved the product qualified rate and productivity. 

In 2005,MCC Machinery passed the ISO9001:2000 international quality system certification.

In April of 2005,MCC Machinery was named the “garden unit” of high-tech development zone, Zhengzhou city. 

In June of 2005,high-tech development zone, Zhengzhou city became the academician of China Gravel Association.

In the whole 2005,products made by MCC Machinery made sales breakthrough one after another in South-East Asia, Africa, Latin America, Eastern Europe, Middle East and other regions. 

In June of 2006,MCC Machinery was named “advanced member” by China Gravel Association, and the overall ranking of MCC products and equipment is “superior”. 

In the whole 2006,the sales of products made by MCC Machinery in international market had grown steadily, and the market feedback was good. 

In March of 2007,MCC Machinery passed the European Union CE certification.

In June of 2007,MCC Machinery gained a bulk order from United States customers, so that its products entered markets in North America and other developed regions. 

In September of 2007,MCC Machinery passed the GOST (gosudarstvennyy standart) formulated by the Russian Federation.

In March of 2008,MCC Machinery gained the title of “Zhengzhou innovative production and management unit”.

In June of 2008,MCC Machinery aligned with the fourth design and research institute of Ministry of Machine Building to form “mutual assistance between institute and enterprise”, to promote deep integration of research and scientific production. 

In 2009,MCC Machinery made university-industry cooperation with Engineering Institute of Zhengzhou University, Mechanics Institute of Henan University of Technology, and Physics Institute of Henan University of Science and Technology, achieving a good combination of teaching and practice. 

In 2010,MCC Machinery gained the “Outstanding Contribution Award of Foreign Enterprise in Native Place” awarded by South Africa’s Chamber of Mines.

In 2011,MCC Machinery was awarded the “Top Ten Outstanding Contribution Award” from Henan Provincial Government, and Henan Provincial Government called for the Henan machinery industry manufacturers in Henan province to learn from the top ten companies.

Since 2012,According to the data from “Report on imports and exports of construction machinery market in China”, the exports of MCC Machinery ranked top three in mining machinery industry for 5 consecutive years, and the annual sales in 2012 also reached 490 million Yuan.
